# Bibliography

Preliminary bibliography.

## Temporal reasoning

- Allen, J. F. (1983). Maintaining knowledge about temporal intervals.
- Pnueli, A. (1977). The temporal logic of programs.
- Lamport, L. (1994). The temporal logic of actions.
- Alur, R., & Dill, D. (1994). A theory of timed automata.

## Multi-agent systems

- Hewitt, C. (1973). A universal modular actor formalism.
- Wooldridge, M. (2009). An introduction to multiagent systems.

## Conversation and interaction

- Sacks, H., Schegloff, E. A., & Jefferson, G. (1974). A simplest systematics for the organization of turn-taking for conversation.
- Clark, H. H. (1996). Using Language.

## Music interaction and improvisation

- Rowe, R. (1993). Interactive Music Systems.
- Pachet, F. Work on the Continuator.
- Pressing, J. (1988). Improvisation: methods and models.
- Lewis, G. E. Work on improvisation and interactive systems.
- Lerdahl, F., & Jackendoff, R. (1983). A Generative Theory of Tonal Music.
- Narmour, E. (1990). The Analysis and Cognition of Basic Melodic Structures.
